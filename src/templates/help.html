Help & User Guide

Learn how to use the AI Legal Judgement Analyzer to analyze court judgements, detect legal issues, and generate reports.

Step 1 — Open the Analysis Page

Click on Analyze in the navigation bar to open the judgement analysis page. This is where you submit the judgement for AI analysis.

Step 2 — Provide the Judgement

You can provide the court judgement in two ways:

  • Paste Text — Copy and paste the judgement text into the text box.
  • Upload PDF — Upload a court judgement PDF file.
Step 3 — Run AI Analysis

Click the Analyze Judgement button. The system will process the judgement using Artificial Intelligence and legal retrieval methods.

Step 4 — View Analysis Results

The system will automatically detect:

  • Main case category (Civil / Criminal)
  • Nature of the legal dispute
  • Relevant legal provisions
  • Important legal issues in the case
  • Confidence score of the classification
Step 5 — Save and Review Cases

Every analyzed judgement is automatically saved to your account. You can view them later in the My Cases or Dashboard section.

Step 6 — Export the Report

You can export the analysis report as a PDF document from the case page. This report includes the case category, legal provisions, and detected issues.

Step 7 — Manage Your Cases
  • View full analysis results
  • Delete cases if needed
  • Export reports
